Shopping

Aug 17th, 2008

Went grocery shopping at Safeway for the first time today. We regularly shop at Bloom, in Herndon, a smaller grocery store. Chrysta and I made the decision that Bloom is not only likely to be less expensive for us to shop at, and that Bloom’s personal scanners are a compelling feature when shopping with small children. I’ll still use Safeway when running small errands while picking up Augusta from preschool (its comfortably on the way by bicycle), but not for regular shopping.
