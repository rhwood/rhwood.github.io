Gardening

May 16th, 2007

I am an Intellipedia contributor on the JWICS network, and have recently begun hunting down and killing Categories that are typographical errors, such as moving two articles from “Army” (2 pages) to “Armies” (40-something pages and an unknown number of subcategories) and one article from “airport” (1 PDF document) to “Airports” (41 pages and I don’t know how many pictures and subcategories).

On Intellipedia, we call this “gardening.” Chrysta and Augusta are starting a different kind of Garden out on our balcony.
